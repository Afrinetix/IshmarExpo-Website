About Ishmar Halal Traders Expo

Africa's Halal Business
Ecosystem Enabler

ISHMAR HALAL TRADERS EXPO LTD is a Kenyan company founded in 2023, dedicated to promoting inclusive economic development through Halal-compliant business exhibitions, corporate events, certification support, and entrepreneurship empowerment programs.

We serve as the bridge between African SMEs and the global Halal economy, connecting businesses, youth, and women entrepreneurs with visibility, training, partnerships, investment opportunities, and Halal certification access.
