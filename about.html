Our Foundation

Built on Purpose,
Driven by Impact

ISHMAR HALAL TRADERS EXPO LTD was founded in October 2023 in Malindi, Kenya, born from a bold vision to promote inclusive economic development through Halal-compliant business exhibitions, corporate events, certification support, and entrepreneurship empowerment.

Our first major milestone came on 5 June 2024, when the Nairobi Ishmar Traders Expo attracted over 500 businesses and 10,000+ attendees, with international speakers, and was officially launched by Esther Muthoni Passaris.

We are the only company in Kenya combining Halal compliance expertise, corporate Halal event management, market access solutions, and inclusive entrepreneurship empowerment under one roof.

Our Purpose

Mission & Vision

M

Our Mission

To support the global Halal economy through Halal certification access, exhibition platforms, corporate Halal event solutions, and business empowerment programs, using ethical and inclusive practices that uplift SMEs, youth, and women-led businesses.

V

Our Vision

To become Africa's leading Halal exhibition and empowerment company, supporting entrepreneurial success globally, serving as the bridge between African SMEs and the world's rapidly growing Halal economy.

Our Journey

From Malindi to Africa's Halal Stage

Oct 2023
Founded in Malindi, Kenya

ISHMAR HALAL TRADERS EXPO LTD was established in Malindi, Kenya with a bold vision: to promote inclusive economic development through Halal-compliant business exhibitions and entrepreneurship empowerment.

Jun 2024
First Flagship Event: Nairobi

The inaugural Nairobi Ishmar Traders Expo on 5 June 2024 was a landmark success, attracting 500+ businesses, 10,000+ attendees, and international speakers. Officially launched by Esther Muthoni Passaris.

2024
Building the Halal Ecosystem

Expanded services to include Halal certification guidance, vendor registration, and market access programs, linking 50+ businesses to Halal certification opportunities across Kenya.

2024–25
Empowerment at Scale

Trained over 1,000 youth and entrepreneurs through dedicated empowerment forums, mentorship programs, and thought leadership panels. Built a community where 60% of exhibitors are women-owned businesses.

2025
Growing Partnerships

Forged 10+ institutional partnerships with governments, development organizations, faith-based organizations, and global Halal trade bodies to support affordable entrepreneurship platforms for SMEs.

2026+
The Next Chapter

Expanding the Halal Traders Expo platform across Africa, seeking government and development organization partnerships to make entrepreneurship expos more accessible and affordable for every SME on the continent.
