FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register as an exhibitor at an Ishmar Halal Traders Expo?
To register as an exhibitor, fill out the contact form above or WhatsApp us on +254 721 639 164. Our team will send you a comprehensive exhibitor pack with booth options, pricing, and registration forms. We welcome SMEs, youth-led and women-led businesses of all sizes.
How does ISHMAR help businesses get Halal certified?
We provide guidance, consultation, and connections to accredited Halal certification bodies. Through our events and dedicated certification programs, we have helped 50+ businesses achieve Halal certification. Contact us to start your Halal certification journey — it is the gateway to global Halal markets.
Can I attend events as a visitor and do I need to pay?
Yes! ISHMAR events are designed to be inclusive and accessible. Many events offer free or affordable visitor registration, especially for youth and community members. Our mission is to remove barriers to participation — contact us for current event pricing and registration details.
What empowerment programs does ISHMAR offer?
We run youth entrepreneurship forums, women-led business platforms, mentorship sessions, skills training workshops, and thought leadership panels. We have trained 1,000+ youth and entrepreneurs. Contact us to find out about upcoming programs or to partner in delivering empowerment training.
How can governments and NGOs partner with ISHMAR?
ISHMAR actively seeks partnerships with governments and development organizations to support affordable SME expo participation. Many small businesses cannot afford exhibitor fees — we welcome co-funding, subsidized participation, and institutional partnership arrangements. Email info@ishmarexpo.com or WhatsApp us to discuss partnership opportunities.
Can ISHMAR manage our private corporate Halal event?
Yes! In addition to our flagship public expos, we offer complete corporate Halal event management — from conferences and product launches to gala dinners and faith-based organizational events. Contact us with your brief and we'll respond with a tailored Halal-compliant proposal.
